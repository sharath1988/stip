The STIP Application processes interest calculations and distributions to a Fund which is shared across various Departments every quarter.

This Application produces Summary Reports and Department Reports for Users.

Instructions for STIP Administrators

The administration link allows you to:

  • Upload GA Excel Workbook
  • Perform Audits
  • Manage Missing and Inactive Indices and Export as Excel for reference to Manage Stip Depts/Indexes
  • Manage Stip Depts/Indexes
  • Update Document Number
  • Reverse Journal
  • View Journals and Export Journals as Excel and Text files
  • Manage Stip Users, Roles and Departments for DBO
  • Generate Quarterly Detail and Fiscal Year Reports and Export Reports as Excel
